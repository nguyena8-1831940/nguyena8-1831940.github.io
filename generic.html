About Us

Stonks was conceived through a simple philosophy of providing full service technical solutions for businesses in the Pacific Northwest. Our hands-on support staff have provided background solutions for these growing firms so that they may "simply do business".

Our mission is to become the leading full-service provider of Information Technology Consulting to businesses large or small. We will provide these services with the highest level of Quality, Integrity, and Satisfaction to our customers

We realize that nurturing your business is not simple or easy, and without an IT staff to support your needs, just the simple day to day endeavors of your business can become mind-boggling. Let us take care of your technical demands so that you and your staff can simply run your business.
